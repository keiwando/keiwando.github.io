Cookie Policy

Updated: 16 September 2018

Does keiwando.com use Cookies?

Almost none of the pages on this website use cookies, so in the vast majority of cases, browsing and interacting with the site will not create any cookies. However, some pages can require cookies in order to offer their basic functionality. As soon as you visit a page which requires cookies or similar technologies such as HTML local storage, you are notified and are given the option to leave the page before any persistent data is stored on your device (Depending on the local laws of your country such a notification might not be displayed). If you continue to use that functionality, you automatically also consent to the use of cookies.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are text files that contain small amounts of information about some of your interactions with a website. These cookies are created by the website and stored locally on your computer. They allow the site to improve your user experience for example by remembering certain actions you have already taken before, so that you don't have to unnecessarily repeat them. For example, if you acknowledge the use of cookies, your decision is remembered in a cookie and the cookie notification is not shown to you during future site visits.

You can find more information on cookies at www.allaboutcookies.org.

How can you change your cookie preferences?

You can usually disable, delete and reenable cookies from within your preferred browser's preferences. https://cookies.insites.com/disable-cookies/ offers step by step instructions for most of the major browsers and operating systems.

This Cookie Policy might be updated from time to time. You will be notified of significant changes but you should also regularly check back in order to keep up to date with the most recent Policy.
